===Player===
<!-- introductory description of player character or units/forces player commands; quotes from game intro or manual are very welcome -->
<!-- additional shortage in table, adjust if needed -->
One to six soldier squad, depending on the level. Squad can be divided max up to 3 groups: with snake logo, eagle or puma. Commanding one of them leaves the other in auto defense mode. In some missions operating a vehicle is required or optional.<br>Every successful mission grants additional 15 soldiers, up to 360.

*None. Single shot or any explosion kill.

====Moves====
'''Basic Moves'''
<!-- bullet list -->
*Attack (single or constant fire)
*Move Forward (one speed)
*Swim (shallow water - shooting possible; deep water - no attack possible)
*Fall Damage: small hills/cliffs - none; high cliffs and chasms - death<br />

|KILL ALL ENEMY

|DESTROY ENEMY BUILDINGS
|frequent<br><small>(most levels)</small>
|blow up all buildings with doors from which the enemy emerge using grenade or bazooka (in very rare circumstances: building with blown up door remains active and enemies still are appearing - blow it up second time)

| PROTECT ALL CIVILIANS
|rare<br><small>(6 levels)</small>
|complete all other level objectives with no civilian casualties

|very rare<br><small>(2 levels)</small>
|destroy barricade to free a civilian then ensure that they make it home alive (no need to move troopers near them to engage or tag them like in "rescue hostages" missions; civilian will relocate towards their home when the path is clear)
|-
|RESCUE HOSTAGES
|rare<br><small>(7 levels)</small>
|kill the captors detaining the hostages, then move troopers near the hostage to ‘tag’ him (or her); the hostage will then follow the troopers; hostage needs to be taken to to the nearest friendly MASH outpost (tent with red cross)
|-

|activate all oval shaped floor buttons - one soldier must stand and stay on the buton until others will stand on the the remaining buttons to activate it;    (lights up or blink red)

|KIDNAP ENEMY LEADER
|unique<br><small>(1 level)</small>
